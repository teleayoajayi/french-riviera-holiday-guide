Monaco

Small but brimming with culture, there is no shortage of art, history, or food here. Monaco is home to the Monte Carlo Casino, one of the most famous casinos in the world. If you're feeling lucky, you're more than welcome to try your luck at its slot machines—or if not, visit its many cafes and restaurants. Or perhaps you enjoy your food with a view? There are a plethora of delicacies to taste at many of Monaco’s Rocher seaside restaurants, bordered by some of the world’s most spectacular yachts.

Must Do's in Monaco

  • Visit Monte Carlo Casino the most famous casino in the world
  • Admire the sea life at Oceanographic Museum of Monaco
  • Visit the official residence of the Sovereign Prince of Monaco, The Prince’s Palace of Monaco
