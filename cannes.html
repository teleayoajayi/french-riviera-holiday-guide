Cannes

If you're looking for somewhere to max out your credit card then cool off on a sunbed, Cannes is the place for you. The town is a perfect amalgamation of retail therapy and beaches—what more could you ask for? Cannes is also a film lover's paradise; as the world capital of film, it is home to one of the most prestigious events in cinema, the International Cannes Film Festival. Every May, the town becomes a hub for Hollywood’s greatest actors and fans to celebrate the art of cinema.

Must Do's in Cannes

  • Rue d'Antibes is the busiest shopping street in Cannes, lined with trendy shops
  • Palais des Festivals is the venue for Cannes annual film festival
  • La Croisette Boulevardstretches over for 3km full of sandy beaches, shops and flamboyant parties
