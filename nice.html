Nice

Founded by the Greeks in the third century BC, Nice is the historical and cultural capital of the French Riviera. It sits on the beautiful pebbly shores of the Baei de Anges and is packed with breathtaking beaches, vibrant old town, markets to patronise and top rated restaurants to sample Provencal food. Sampling socca in this area is a must and a beloved meal of the natives.

Must Do's in Nice

  • Walk along Promenade des Anglaise
  • Admire the Orthodox church Cathedral St Nicholas
  • Walk through the enchanting cobbled streets of Nice' Old Town
